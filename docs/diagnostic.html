Safety and evidence boundaries

The diagnostic is analysis only. It does not include production changes, restarts, retries, sends, writes, deletions, implementation, monitoring, penetration testing, or a guarantee that every hidden issue will be found.

Do not send credentials, customer records, or unredacted production logs. Tests use synthetic or client-approved sanitized cases. Scope, evidence, timing, and payment terms are confirmed in writing before work begins.
